# UFTS (University Finance Tracker System) - User Guide 🚀

Welcome to **UFTS**! This application is designed to help university students and faculty easily track and manage their personal finances, budgets, and daily expenses.

Follow this guide to get started and make the most out of the platform.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

Open your web browser and navigate to the application URL (e.g., `http://localhost/finance-tracker/`).

### Registration

If you don't have an account yet:

1. Click on **Register** on the login page.
2. Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password) and select your role (e.g., Student).
3. Click **Create Account**.

### Logging In

1. Enter your registered email address and password.
2. Check **"Remember Me"** if you want to stay logged in on your personal device.
3. Click **Login** to access your dashboard.

_(Note: For security, your account will temporarily lock after 5 failed login attempts)._

---

## 2. Navigating the Dashboard

Once logged in, you will be greeted by the **Dashboard**. This is your financial command center:

- **Summary Cards:** Quickly view your Total Income, Total Expenses, Current Balance, and Active Budgets.
- **Budget Health:** Real-time progress bars show how close you are to reaching your budget limits.
- **Recent Activity:** A quick feed of your latest transactions.

**Top Navigation & Sidebar:**

- Use the sidebar (on the left) to navigate between Dashboard, Transactions, Budgets, Reports, and Insights.
- Toggle **Dark Mode** using the moon/sun icon in the top navigation bar.
- Use the **Keyboard Shortcut `D`** from anywhere to instantly return to the Dashboard.

---

## 3. Managing Transactions

Transactions are your daily incomes and expenses.

### Adding a Transaction

1. Navigate to the **Transactions** page from the sidebar (or press **`N`** on your keyboard as a shortcut).
2. Click **Add Transaction**.
3. Select the **Type** (Income or Expense).
4. Enter the **Amount**, choose a **Category** (e.g., Food, Tuition, Salary), and pick the **Date**.
5. Add an optional description to remind yourself what the transaction was for.
6. Click **Save**.

### Viewing and Filtering

- The Transactions table allows you to **Search** for specific entries live without reloading the page.
- You can **Filter** by Category, Type (Income/Expense), or Date Range.
- Click the column headers to **Sort** your data.

---

## 4. Setting Up Budgets

Budgets help you keep your spending in check.

1. Go to **Budgets** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Create Budget**.
3. Select a category (e.g., Entertainment) and set a monthly **Spending Limit**.
4. Save the budget.
5. As you add expenses in that category, your budget progress bar will fill up. The system will warn you if you are getting close to your limit!

---

## 5. Reports & Analytics

Want to know where your money is going?

1. Click on **Reports** in the sidebar.
2. Here, you can view beautifully designed charts (pie charts and bar graphs) showing your spending breakdown.
3. Adjust the date filters to see reports for specific months or semesters.
4. You can export your financial records to a **CSV file** for your own spreadsheet records.

---

## 6. Smart Insights

The **Insights** tab acts as your personal financial advisor:

- It analyzes your spending patterns over time.
- Warns you about unusual spending spikes.
- Tracks your "Saving Streaks" (consecutive days without unnecessary expenses) to keep you motivated!

---

## 7. Profile & Settings

Customize your UFTS experience:

1. Click your avatar in the top right corner and select **Profile**.
2. **Update Avatar:** Upload a profile picture.
3. **Currency Preferences:** Change your preferred currency display (e.g., USD, GBP, UGX).
4. **Change Password:** Securely update your password if needed.

---

## 8. Mobile App Experience (PWA)

You don't need to visit the browser every time. You can install UFTS directly to your phone or computer!

1. Open UFTS in your mobile browser (Chrome/Safari).
2. Tap the browser menu and select **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**.
3. UFTS will now appear as a standalone app on your device, complete with an icon and offline capabilities!
